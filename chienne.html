'Chienne de vie' ('Dog's Life') is a subjective brand with the goal of selling alcohol in small but effective amounts. This brand believes that alcohol is the solution to problems and should be made easily accessible. The alcohol is contained in small glass bottles, meant to be consumed in one shot after opening.
